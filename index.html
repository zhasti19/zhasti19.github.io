Welcome

Hi! I'm Zachary Hastings, a biological engineering student at Utah State University.

The purpose of this site is to share what I've learned about Synthetic Biology in an accessible format for the public. This site is a place for people from all walks of life to learn about Synthetic Biology. Here there is information from the fundamentals of biology all the way to simple explanations of some of the latest reasearch in the Synthetic Biology.
